Our Story

A Legacy of Love

Nestled continuously on 500 acres of rolling Texas hill country, Grand Oak Estates has been the backdrop for unforgettable moments for over three decades. Our historic estate combines the warmth of a rustic barn with the sophistication of a modern luxury venue.

Whether you dream of an intimate garden ceremony under our centenary oaks or a grand reception in our crystal-chandelier ballroom, our dedicated team ensures every detail reflects your unique love story.
